Event Guidelines

Participation

• All employees attending the offsite are encouraged to participate in activities.

• Team captains will help coordinate participation.

Attendance

• If someone is unable to attend after confirming, please inform the organizers beforehand.

Respect and inclusivity.

• The offsite is meant to be a safe and inclusive environment for everyone.

• Any behavior that is disrespectful, discriminatory, or inappropriate will not be tolerated.

Personal belongings

• Participants are responsible for their own personal belongings.

• The company or organizers will not be liable for any lost items.

Cleanliness

• Please help maintain cleanliness at the venue.

• Dispose of waste in designated bins.

Conduct

• Follow instructions given by volunteers and organizers.

Safety

• Follow safety instructions during physical activities.

• Inform your group leader if you have any medical condition or injury.

Timelines

• Activities will follow the planned itinerary schedule.

• Participants are expected to arrive on time.

Equipment

• Any event equipment should be used responsibly.

• Avoid damaging or misplacing event materials.

Cash loss

• If any employee cancels their travel ticket after it has been booked, the cancellation charges will have to be borne by the employee.

FAQs

Q1. When is the offsite and onsite happening?

The offsite will take place from 2nd April to 5th April. The onsite sessions will continue from 6th April to 9th April.

Q2. What should we bring for the offsite?

  • Comfortable clothing
  • Sports shoes for activities
  • Swimming costume for the pool party
  • A pair of blue jeans for the group photograph
  • Official ID proof (such as PAN card or Aadhaar card)
  • Any personal essentials you may need during the trip

Q2. What should we bring for the onsite?

Please ensure you carry the following items for a smooth and productive onsite experience:

  • Laptop and charger
  • Official / smart casual clothes for work sessions
  • Comfortable clothes for travel or informal activities
  • Personal toiletries and essentials
  • Any required medications
  • Notebook and pen (optional, for quick notes)
  • Phone charger / power bank
  • Government ID proof (if required for travel or hotel check-in)

Make sure all important work-related devices and chargers are packed before departure.

Q3. Are activities mandatory?

Participation is encouraged as the activities are designed for team bonding. However, people with health concerns may opt out.

Q4. Will teams be pre-decided?

It depends on the activity; teams will be formed in advance and shared before the activities begin.

Q5. What kind of activities are planned?

Some activities include:

  • Movie / entertainment session
  • Pictionary (Draw the opposite)
  • Obstacle course
  • The VGF show
  • Outdoor games like Cricket
  • Pool hand ball

Q6. Is food included in the event?

Yes, all meals and refreshments will be arranged, and the menu will be completely vegetarian.

Q7. What if someone gets late or cannot attend a specific activity?

Please inform the event coordinators in advance.

Q8. Is there a dress code?

Comfortable casual clothing is recommended. Sports shoes are suggested for outdoor games.
